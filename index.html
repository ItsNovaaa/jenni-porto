Hi, I'm Jenni Pramadani

I have always been intrigued and inspired by the varieties of expression in people and animals. I’m trained in portraiture and traditional techniques, but will often add collage, thick texture, words, and calligraphic marks to my paintings. Ultimately, I’m inspired by the wildness of nature, aiming for a sense of abandon and freedom in my work.
